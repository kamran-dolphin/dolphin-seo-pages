Question 1

Ratio of boys to girls is 3:5, 40 students total. How many girls?

Explanation: Girls = 5/8 × 40 = 25.
Question 2

4 workers build a wall in 6 days. How many days for 8 workers?

Explanation: Inverse: 4×6 = 8×d, d = 3.
Question 3

Map scale: 1 inch = 50 miles. Cities are 3.5 inches apart. Actual distance?

Explanation: 3.5 × 50 = 175 miles.
Question 4

If x/4 = 15/20, what is x?

Explanation: Cross multiply: 20x = 60, x = 3.
Question 5

Recipe for 4 servings uses 3 cups flour. How much for 10 servings?

Explanation: 3/4 = x/10, x = 7.5 cups.

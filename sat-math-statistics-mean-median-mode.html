Question 1

Test scores of 5 students: 72, 85, 90, 88, 95. What is the mean?

Explanation: Sum = 430. Mean = 430/5 = 86.
Question 2

Data set: 3, 7, 7, 10, 15. What is the median?

Explanation: Ordered: 3,7,7,10,15. Middle value = 7.
Question 3

Mean of 4 numbers is 20. Three are 15, 22, 25. What is the fourth?

Explanation: Sum must be 80. 15+22+25=62. Fourth = 18.
Question 4

Explanation: Mean is most affected by outliers.
Question 5

Data set: mean 50, standard deviation 5. ~68% of data falls between?

Explanation: 68% within 1 SD: 45 to 55.

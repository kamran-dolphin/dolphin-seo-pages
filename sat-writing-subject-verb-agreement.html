SAT Subject-Verb Agreement — Practice & Tips

Subject-verb agreement is one of the most tested grammar concepts. The test makes it tricky by hiding the subject behind prepositional phrases and inverted structures.

Your score: 0/0
Question 1

The group of students ___ ready.

Explanation: Subject = 'group' (singular). 'Of students' is a prepositional phrase.
Question 2

Neither the teacher nor the students ___ the answer.

Explanation: With 'neither...nor,' verb matches closest subject: 'students' = know.
Question 3

The results of the experiment ___ surprising.

Explanation: Subject = 'results' (plural).
Question 4

Each of the players ___ a uniform.

Explanation: 'Each' is always singular = has.
Question 5

There ___ many reasons to study for the SAT.

Explanation: Subject = 'reasons' (plural) = are.
